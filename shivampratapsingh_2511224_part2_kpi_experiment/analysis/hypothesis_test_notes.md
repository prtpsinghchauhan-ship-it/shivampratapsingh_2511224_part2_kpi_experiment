# Hypothesis Test Notes

## 1. Hypotheses
* **Null Hypothesis ($H_0$):** The Paid Conversion Rate of the Treatment group is less than or equal to the Control group.
* **Alternate Hypothesis ($H_1$):** The Paid Conversion Rate of the Treatment group is greater than the Control group.

## 2. Test Details
* **Test Type:** One-tailed Two-Proportion Z-Test
* **Significance Level:** 0.05
* **Metric Tested:** Paid Conversion Rate (chosen because it directly measures the core business goal of acquiring paying subscribers).

## 3. Results & Interpretation
The calculated p-value is 0.000573. Since this value is less than our 0.05 significance level, we reject the Null Hypothesis. We have statistical evidence that the Treatment group conversion rate is higher than the Control. However, segment analysis reveals this success is isolated to specific platforms and comes with operational risks.
